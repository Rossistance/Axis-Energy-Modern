O&M Basic Requirements

Environmental Health & Safety

EH&S (Environmental Health & Safety) – Team members need to be acquainted with recognizing environmental problems and potential problems.

It is also crucial that a Health and Safety Policy and Safety Training Matrix be on file and weekly company wide meetings be established to ensure that staff are aware of and knowledgeable in the company’s policies pertaining to the safety and welfare of its employees.

The EH&S Policy is managed by a Safety Coordinator/Manager who ensures all training documentation, procedures, incident reporting and all other related documentation be kept and maintained.

Plant operations

Power Plant Operation

Power Plant Operation – This is the remote monitoring, data acquisition service operation, and control of the plant.

Coordination

It involves good planning and coordination with the corrective and preventive maintenance teams for the timely shut down of equipment, repairs, outage response, adherence to PPA stipulations, documentation of all personnel that enter and exit the plant.

Documentation

Documentation control and management is crucial to operating and maintaining a plant successfully.

Communication

Site security, emergency response, outage alerts, trending data and other data acquisition derived data will be documented and communicated by this person or group to constantly improve upon plant performance and good stewardship of the plant assets.

Maintenance focus

Personnel and Training

Team members should be trained and have the relevant qualifications to perform the work in a safe, accountable manner that is in accordance with the company’s HASP (Health And Safety Program).

Technicians should be well versed in the navigation of this program and be encouraged to be very descriptive in their reporting and documentation, such as pictures, inverter error codes, etc.

A CMMS program will be needed for tracking, documenting, reporting and planning maintenance work, outage service response, incidents, etc.

An “On Call” plan will need to be established to ensure all required response times for outages (which vary by contract) be fulfilled.
