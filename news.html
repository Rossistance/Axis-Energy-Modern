Global perspective June 14, 2018

Solar could provide 25% of the world’s energy by 2050

Much of the solar conversation in America this year has been focused on market forces slowing growth.

Here are a few examples of how solar is deployed around the world:

  • China is now the biggest market for new solar generation.
  • India plans to reach 100GW of solar capacity by 2022.
  • Saudi Arabia, the leader of OPEC sees the value of solar power.

The International Energy Agency projects that by 2050 the world could be getting over 25% of its energy from solar power.
